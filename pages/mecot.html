About the Token

MECOT is a BEP-20 token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain. It enables on-chain voting, airdrop rewards, and serves as the utility token for my Medium community educating people about the crypto world.

AI, crypto and writing are my passion. I thus started a Medium profile sharing knowledge about the crypto space. But is becoming more than a profile - it is a community. This token simultaneously serves as a learning experience - members of the community can follow me on the journey of creating a real-valued crypto token - as well as means to interact with the community.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000 MECOT
  • 50% Liquidity Pool
  • 30% Airdrops and investors
  • 20% Team Wallet (vested)

How It Works

Holders of MECOT can participate in on-chain governance by voting on proposals as well as sharing ideas, via which the community can interact with me and each other. The token is mintable and supports airdrop distribution and burn mechanisms. Vote weight is determined by token balance.
