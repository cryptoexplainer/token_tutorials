About the Token

MECOT is a BEP-20 token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain. It enables on-chain voting, airdrop rewards, and serves as the utility token for my Medium community educating people about the crypto world.

AI, crypto and writing are my passion. I thus started a Medium profile sharing knowledge about the crypto space. But it is becoming more than a profile - it is a community. This token simultaneously serves as a learning experience - members of the community can follow me on the journey of creating a real-valued crypto token - as well as means to interact with the community.
